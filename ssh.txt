SSH
Disable root login in /etc/ssh/sshd_config
	-PermitRootLogin yes
	to
	-PermitRootLogin no

Disable private key login in /etc/ssh/sshd_config
	-PubkeyAuthentication yes 
	to
	-PubkeyAuthentication no
